Q: What is Madridejos Community College (MCC)?
A: Madridejos Community College (MCC) is a public community college located in Bunakan, Madridejos, Cebu, Philippines. It provides quality higher education programs and serves the educational needs of the local community and surrounding areas.

Q: Where is MCC located?
A: MCC is located at Bunakan, Madridejos, Cebu, Philippines. The campus is situated near the Madridejos Public Market and Municipal Hall, making it easily accessible to students and visitors.

Q: What academic programs does MCC offer?
A: MCC offers the following CHED-accredited programs:
• BSIT (Bachelor of Science in Information Technology) - Focus on programming, networking, AI, and software development
• BSBA (Bachelor of Science in Business Administration) - Specializations in marketing, finance, and entrepreneurship
• BEED (Bachelor of Elementary Education) - Prepares future elementary school teachers
• BSED (Bachelor of Secondary Education) - Specializations in English, Math, and Science
• BSHM (Bachelor of Science in Hospitality Management) - Focus on hotel operations, tourism, and event management

Q: What are the admission requirements for MCC?
A: To apply for admission to MCC, you need:
1. Completed application form
2. Form 138 (Report Card/Transcript of Records)
3. PSA Birth Certificate (original and photocopy)
4. 2x2 ID pictures (recent)
5. Good Moral Certificate from previous school
6. Registration fee payment

Q: How much are the tuition fees at MCC?
A: MCC offers tuition-free education as a public community college. Students only need to pay:
• Registration fee for first-year students: ₱100 (for Student Supreme Council/SSC)
• Miscellaneous fees may apply for specific courses or activities
For detailed fee information, contact the Registrar's Office at registrar@mcc-nac.edu.ph

Q: What campus facilities are available at MCC?

• Science Laboratories - For education and other science-related courses

Q: What scholarships are available at MCC?
A: MCC offers various scholarship opportunities:
• CHED Grants - For qualified students based on academic merit and financial need
• TES (Tertiary Education Subsidy) - Government financial assistance program
• LGU Scholarships - For Madridejos residents and neighboring municipalities
• Academic Scholarships - For students with outstanding academic performance
• Indigenous Peoples (IP) Scholarships - For qualified IP students
Contact the Registrar's Office for application procedures and requirements.

Q: What is the academic calendar for MCC?
A: The typical academic calendar follows:
• First Semester: August - December
• Second Semester: January - May
• Summer Term: June - July (optional, for some programs)
• Enrollment periods are announced before each semester
Check the MCC-NAC Portal or contact the Registrar's Office for exact dates and schedules.

Q: How do I enroll at MCC?
A: The enrollment process includes:
1. Submit admission requirements to the Registrar's Office
2. Take entrance examination (if required)
3. Attend orientation for new students
4. Complete registration and pay fees
5. Receive class schedules and student ID
6. Attend first day of classes
For specific enrollment dates and procedures, visit the campus or check the MCC-NAC Portal.

Q: What career opportunities are available for MCC graduates?
A: MCC graduates pursue various career paths:
• BSIT Graduates: Software Developer, IT Support Specialist, Network Administrator, Database Administrator, Web Developer, Systems Analyst
• BSBA Graduates: Business Manager, Marketing Specialist, Financial Analyst, Entrepreneur, Sales Manager
• BEED/BSED Graduates: Elementary/Secondary School Teacher, Education Supervisor, Curriculum Developer, Private Tutor
• BSHM Graduates: Hotel Manager, Tourism Officer, Event Coordinator, Restaurant Manager, Travel Agent

Q: Does MCC offer online or distance learning programs?
A: MCC adapts to modern educational needs and offers flexible learning modalities:
• Face-to-face classes (traditional classroom setting)
• Blended learning (combination of online and face-to-face)
• Online classes (when necessary, such as during emergencies)
The specific modality depends on the program and current circumstances. Contact the academic departments for current offerings.

Q: How can I get academic transcripts or certificates from MCC?
A: To request academic documents:
1. Visit the Registrar's Office during office hours
2. Fill out the request form for transcripts/certificates
3. Pay the required fees
4. Provide valid identification
5. Wait for processing time (usually 3-5 working days)
6. Claim documents with proper receipt
For urgent requests, inquire about expedited processing options.
